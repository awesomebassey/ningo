Ningo is a language learning app for Nigerian languages and was developed by Maradi Studio, the brand that is now gaining popularity for developing mobile apps using android phones.

The app was developed for the 60 Days App Challenge organized by the Federal Ministry of Youth and Sports Development and was categorized under the EduTech section of the Challenge.

The aim of the app is to preserve the Nigerian culture and make the learning of Nigerian languages fun through games. It features 5 major Nigerian languages: Hausa, Ibo, Yoruba, Edo and Ibibio. Other languages will be included from time to time.
